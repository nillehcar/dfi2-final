Services

  • Print

    Capital Printers offers printing on various types of plastic, plastic, and cardboard. We make business cards, menus, flyers, pamphlets and much more. We are capable of printing large format sizes and photographs. If you have a custom job, feel free to contact us and we will get it done.

    Capital Printers offer silkscreen printing and embroidery on site. These processes can be done on a variety of apparels and materials ranging from cotton shirts to canvas bags. We also offer heat transfers for jerseys, bags, and jackets.

  • Graphic Design

    Turn your idea into a printable or digital graphic. We have an experienced design team on site everyday. Whether it's company branding, promotional event, or just for fun we have the solution to any design you might have.
